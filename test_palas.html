Hotel Palas 4☆

Four star rated resort hotel Palas is situated in Petrovac, a small coastal settlement embraced by rugged hills, mild Mediterranean climate, beaches that combine sand and pebble, and hospitable locals. The hotel itself is centrally located, encircled by orange and olive trees in a cove on the Petrovac Bay, with a view on two islands; Katic and Sveta Nedjelja.

The landmarks of this small town make it the right fit for families, senior citizens, couples, and all who seek an idyllic environment for vacations.

      About Hotel

      Hotel Palas was built in 1983 and renovated in 2011. The overall look is enriched by recently added hotel facilities, as well as a modern wellness and spa center. Considering the location and the offer of the hotel, it is ideal for family holidays, MICE tourism, wedding ceremonies, cocktails and many other events.

      Hotel Palas is located right on the seashore, with direct access to the promenade along which numerous interesting attractions can be found. Previous guests praise it as "the hotel with the most beautiful view in the world", and with only 18 kilometers to Budva and 10 km from St. Stefan, Palas is the epicenter of all tourist activities on the coast of Montenegro.

      Palas LUX hotel, a dependence of the Palas hotel, exudes a modern interior and high-quality accommodation capacities. The service offered is bed and breakfast. Guests can use other services and sports and recreational facilities at the Palas hotel.

      ABOUT HOTEL

      HOTEL PALAS - FAVOURITE VENUE IN PETROVAC

      The outside of the hotel is surrounded by a lot of green areas of our cultivated garden and an open terrace which, during the summer, becomes the most popular place for various entertainment activities: from folklore to salsa evenings, ethno-mediterranean music concerts, theatres for children, street entertainers, as well as a lot of other entertaining content. Our guests have the advantage of attending all events that take place within the hotel free of charge.

      If you are a fan of water activities, there are numerous options in Petrovac such as kayaking, fishing and diving. This charming town also entertains with music, sports, art and culture, especially when it traditionally hosts the "Petrovac Night" event.

      NEW YEAR IN HOTEL PALAS

      Apart from serving delight and fun during sunny seasons, Hotel Palas additionally organises a spectacular New Year's Eve in Petrovac, which is one of the most attractive ones in Montenegro and the Balkan region. The event is covered with great music performed by big-name singers, delicious continental food prepared by gourmet culinary chefs, and lots of laughter during the whole celebration.
