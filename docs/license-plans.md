## How to Activate Your License

1. After purchasing on Gumroad, you receive a license key by email. Format: `xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx`.
2. Log in to your Joomla administrator panel.
3. Go to **System → Manage → Plugins**, find **JoomlaBoost**, and click to open settings.
4. On the **Plugin** tab, under **License**, paste your key into the **License Key** field.
5. Click **Save**.

The field displays a **Licensed** badge with your plan tier (Starter, Developer, or Agency) after a successful validation.

> **Key format:** UUID v4 — 8-4-4-4-12 hex characters separated by hyphens. The key is validated against the Gumroad API on save.

## Using JoomlaBoost on Multiple Sites

- **Starter** — license is valid for one production domain. Using the same key on a second site will work at the code level, but violates the license terms.
- **Developer** — up to 5 unique domains. Ideal for freelancers and small agencies managing a handful of client sites.
- **Agency** — unlimited domains. Suitable for hosting companies and agencies with many client sites.
