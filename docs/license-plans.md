## How to Activate Your License

1. After purchase, you receive a license key by email. Format: `xxxxxxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxx-xxxxxxxxxxxx`.
2. Log in to your Joomla administrator panel.
3. Go to **System → Manage → Plugins**, find **AI Boost for Joomla**, and click to open settings.
4. In the **Setup** area, under **License**, paste your key into the **License Key** field.
5. Click **Save**.

The field displays a **Licensed** badge after a successful validation.

> **Key format:** UUID v4 — 8-4-4-4-12 hex characters separated by hyphens.
