# Licence & Plans

**AI Boost for Joomla** comes in a **Free** edition and a **Pro Upgrade** add-on. This page explains the Pro plans, how activation works, and exactly what happens when a licence expires.

---

## Plans & Pricing

All Pro plans include **identical Pro features**. They differ only in the number of sites you can activate.

| Plan | Sites | Price |
| --- | --- | --- |
| **AI Boost PRO** | 3 sites | EUR 65 / year |
| **AI Boost Pro+** | 10 sites | EUR 120 / year |
| **AI Boost Unlimited** | unlimited sites | EUR 180 / year |

Plans are yearly subscriptions, sold through **Lemon Squeezy** at [aiboostnow.com/pricing](https://aiboostnow.com/pricing). Lemon Squeezy acts as Merchant of Record and handles EU VAT and invoicing for you — your receipt and licence key arrive by e-mail right after purchase.

## Perpetual activation — the core promise

Once your licence key is verified on a site, **Pro features keep working on that site forever.**

- **An expired licence never re-locks features.** If you let the subscription lapse, everything you configured continues to run exactly as before.
- Expiry only **pauses updates and support**: you stop receiving new Pro releases and support eligibility ends. Renewing restores both.
- The activation also **survives uninstall and reinstall** on the same site — if you remove the package and install it again later, the site comes back activated (see the [Uninstall Guide](uninstall-guide.md)).

In the admin panel this is shown on **SETUP → License & Updates**: an activated site displays a permanent "License activated" confirmation, and a lapsed licence shows at most a renewal reminder — never a lock.

## How to activate

1. Purchase a plan at [aiboostnow.com/pricing](https://aiboostnow.com/pricing) — your licence key arrives by e-mail.
2. Install the Free package, then the **Pro Upgrade** package on top (see [Getting Started](getting-started.md)).
3. Go to **Components → AI Boost** → sidebar **SETUP → License & Updates**.
4. Paste the key into the **License key** field and click **Verify**.

The status badge changes to **Active** and the perpetual-activation confirmation appears. The page also shows when the licence was last verified and when the next automatic check is due — you can click **Verify now** at any time.

### Moving a licence to another site

Each plan covers a fixed number of activations. To move one, open **License & Updates** on the old site and click **Release**, then verify the key on the new site. (Releasing a licence does not remove the perpetual activation already granted to the old site.)

## Updates

| Edition | How updates arrive |
| --- | --- |
| **Free** | Joomla's native updater — an "Update available" notice appears under **System → Update → Extensions** |
| **Pro Upgrade** | Through your Lemon Squeezy **My Orders** customer portal, with an e-mail notification for each release. Download the new ZIP and install it over the old version — settings and licence are preserved. |

There is no automatic in-app Pro update — Pro releases are always installed from the ZIP you download from the customer portal.

---

## Renewals, receipts & cancellation

- Subscriptions renew yearly through Lemon Squeezy; you can cancel any time from the customer portal linked in your purchase e-mail.
- After cancellation or expiry your sites keep their Pro features (perpetual activation) — you simply stop receiving updates and support.
- Receipts and EU VAT invoices are issued by Lemon Squeezy and e-mailed to you automatically.
