Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to the most common questions about HG Budvanska Rivijera and our hotels in Budva and Petrovac, Montenegro.

HG Budvanska Rivijera – General

What is HG Budvanska Rivijera?

HG Budvanska Rivijera is the largest hotel group in Montenegro, operating four-star properties in the coastal towns of Budva and Petrovac. Our portfolio includes TN Slovenska Plaža, Hotel Palas, Hotel Palas Lux, Hotel Aleksandar, and Hotel Castellastva.

Where are the HG Budvanska Rivijera hotels located?

Our hotels are located in two Montenegrin coastal towns: Budva (TN Slovenska Plaža and Hotel Aleksandar) and Petrovac na Moru (Hotel Palas, Hotel Palas Lux, and Hotel Castellastva). Both destinations are on the Adriatic coast with direct beach access.

Does HG Budvanska Rivijera offer direct booking?

Yes. Booking directly on hgbudvanskarivijera.com guarantees the lowest available rate and gives you access to exclusive promotions and special offers not available through third-party portals.

What is the standard check-in and check-out time across all hotels?

Check-in is at 14:00 and check-out is at 11:00 across all HG Budvanska Rivijera properties. Early check-in and late check-out are subject to availability and may be arranged upon request.

Can I organise a conference or corporate event at HG Budvanska Rivijera?

Yes. Hotel Palas in Petrovac has a dedicated conference hall suitable for business meetings, seminars, team buildings, and corporate events. Contact us for availability and group packages.

Are the hotels suitable for families with children?

Yes. Hotel Aleksandar in Budva is particularly family-oriented, featuring an animation program and family rooms. TN Slovenska Plaža resort with its 10 villas is also an excellent choice for families.

TN Slovenska Plaža – Budva

What is TN Slovenska Plaža?

TN Slovenska Plaža (Tourist Settlement Slovenska Plaža) is a four-star villa resort in Budva comprising 10 individual villas. Each villa offers comfortable and private accommodation with direct access to Slovenska Plaža, one of the most popular beaches in Montenegro.

How many villas does TN Slovenska Plaža have?

The resort consists of 10 individual villas, providing comfortable and private accommodation for families, groups, and couples.

Is TN Slovenska Plaža directly on the beach?

Yes, TN Slovenska Plaža has direct access to Slovenska Plaža beach in Budva.

What facilities are available at TN Slovenska Plaža?

Guests enjoy direct beach access, a swimming pool, restaurant and bar services, and free WiFi throughout the resort.

How do I book TN Slovenska Plaža?

Book directly on hgbudvanskarivijera.com for the lowest guaranteed rate and exclusive direct booking offers.

Hotel Palas & Hotel Palas Lux – Petrovac

Where is Hotel Palas located?

Hotel Palas is located in Petrovac na Moru, a picturesque town on the Montenegrin Riviera approximately 25 km south of Budva.

What star rating does Hotel Palas have?

Hotel Palas is a four-star hotel offering well-appointed rooms, many with sea views, along with a full range of guest facilities.

Does Hotel Palas have a swimming pool?

Yes, Hotel Palas has an outdoor swimming pool available to all hotel guests.

What is Hotel Palas Lux?

Hotel Palas Lux is the premium luxury annex of Hotel Palas, offering superior rooms and suites with enhanced amenities and personalised service for guests seeking an elevated experience.

Can I hold a conference at Hotel Palas?

Yes, Hotel Palas has a dedicated conference hall that accommodates business meetings, seminars, and corporate gatherings. Please contact us for capacity details and packages.

Is Hotel Palas close to the beach?

Yes, Hotel Palas is conveniently situated near one of Montenegro's most beautiful natural beaches in Petrovac.

Hotel Aleksandar – Budva

Where is Hotel Aleksandar located?

Hotel Aleksandar is located in Budva, Montenegro, within easy reach of the beach and Budva's historic old town.

Is Hotel Aleksandar suitable for families?

Yes, Hotel Aleksandar is a four-star hotel with a strong family focus, offering a structured daily animation program, family rooms, and facilities designed for guests travelling with children.

Does Hotel Aleksandar have an animation program?

Yes, Hotel Aleksandar offers a daily animation program for children of various age groups during the summer season.

Does Hotel Aleksandar have beach access?

Yes, Hotel Aleksandar guests have easy access to nearby beaches in Budva.

Are family rooms available at Hotel Aleksandar?

Yes, Hotel Aleksandar offers family rooms designed to accommodate parents and children comfortably.

Hotel Castellastva – Petrovac

Where is Hotel Castellastva located?

Hotel Castellastva is located in Petrovac na Moru, Montenegro, nestled in lush Mediterranean greenery of pine and olive trees.

Is Hotel Castellastva close to the beach?

Yes, Hotel Castellastva is within easy walking distance of Petrovac beach.

What facilities does Hotel Castellastva offer?

Hotel Castellastva offers comfortable four-star accommodation, an on-site restaurant, garden, and free WiFi for all guests.

Is Hotel Castellastva suitable for a quiet, relaxing holiday?

Yes, the peaceful Mediterranean setting of Hotel Castellastva makes it an ideal choice for guests seeking a tranquil retreat surrounded by nature.

Hotel Mogren – Budva

Is Hotel Mogren currently open?

Hotel Mogren in Budva is currently closed for renovation. We will announce the reopening date as soon as it is confirmed. Please explore our other properties in Budva and Petrovac in the meantime.

Where is Hotel Mogren located?

Hotel Mogren is situated in Budva, Montenegro, near the famous Mogren beaches.

When will Hotel Mogren reopen?

The reopening date has not yet been confirmed. Follow our website and social media for updates.
