MONTENEGRO BEACHES

Some of the best beaches on the Adriatics are located in the heart of the Montenegrin coastline, in a charming little town of Budva. This Mediterranean pearl is famous for its historical heritage, a 38 km long seacoast and beautiful sandy beaches with turquoise crystal clear waters, which make it a must-visit for anyone keen to have a memorable summer experience.

Reimagined & Unique Holidays

Trustworthy Montenegrin Brand

Our hotel group is a distinctive Montenegrin label for family-oriented tourism services, with inventory that’s spread across two coastal settlements: Budva and Petrovac. All estates feature breath-taking vegetation, conference rooms for business meetings, entertainment programmes, direct access to the sea with room views facing either various shades of blue to the front, or rugged hills to the back.

OUR HOTELS

Hotel Palas
Hotel Palas is a family resort in Petrovac with access to beaches that combine sand and pebble. The locality in which the building is situated is surrounded by orange and olive trees, and other green areas that warrant pleasant vacations.
Hotel Palas Lux
Palas Lux Hotel, the Palace Hotel depandance is located in one of the most attractive locations in Petrovac. It exudes a contemporary interior and high-quality accommodation capacities.
Hotel Aleksandar
Ideally located near the beach, yet far away from the major hustle and bustle, lies hotel Aleksandar, a family club hotel that has welcomed guests in Budva since 1989. The complex represents the jewel in the crown of the Hotel Group “Budvanska Rivijera” tourism offers.
Hotel Castellastva
The establishment is located in the centre of Petrovac, a coastal town, snugged between rugged hills and overlooking the Adriatic sea. It is an ideal place for families or elderly guests who look for relaxing vacations in a Mediterranean ambiance.
TN Slovenska Plaža
This hotel is the largest estate of the Hotel Group Budvanska Rivijera and appoints 10 villas in total, all of them forming one beautiful whole. It is connected to the main promenade, where you can find the same-named Slovenska Plaza beach.
Hotel Mogren
Built in 1934 and renovated in 2011, this hotel represents the uphold the tradition of its people. Though many aspects about this hotel are being considered an advantage, one particularly stands out - the terrace that’s facing the walls of the Old Town of Budva.
